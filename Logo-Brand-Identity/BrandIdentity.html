Velvet Kiss

Overview
Designed the logo and visual identity for Velvet Kiss, a bold women’s undergarment brand that celebrates confidence, femininity, and self-expression. The design reflects the brand’s daring yet elegant tone, blending modern sophistication with sensual minimalism. Every element from typography to color palette was crafted to embody empowerment and allure, creating a strong and memorable identity that resonates with the modern, fearless woman.
